Box Office Predictor

By John Kettula

What is this Web Application?

This project was designed to be an independent study, overseen by Dr. Babcock. The main objective of this web application is to experiment and play with Spring Boot, which I will use in my future job after college. The purpose of this application is to be a box office predictor of some sorts. I'm an avid movie watcher and I enjoy watching the box office numbers and seeing what movies are going to reach the top of the box office (I was disappointed to see MI Dead Reckoning only got 551 mil at the box office). Seeing these massive movies with big budgets flop at the box office lead to me question if there was a way to predict box office revenue. I always see articles that 'predict' that a movie will open with '50 million' opening, and I was curious to see if executives had some algorithm that I didn't know about.
